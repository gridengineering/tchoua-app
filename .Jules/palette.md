## 2025-05-18 - Accessibility of Icon-Only Buttons
**Learning:** I discovered a prevalent accessibility issue throughout the application's components: icon-only buttons, particularly those used for closing modals (using the `<X />` icon) or deleting items (using the `<Trash2 />` icon), frequently lack `aria-label` attributes. This makes these interactive elements inaccessible to users relying on screen readers, as the purpose of the buttons is conveyed solely through visual icons.
**Action:** Always ensure that icon-only buttons include an appropriate `aria-label` attribute (e.g., `aria-label="Fermer"`, `aria-label="Supprimer"`) to provide clear and descriptive text for assistive technologies. Establish a habit of searching for `<button>` elements that only wrap icons and proactively adding these labels.
